Meet Our

Physician

Restore Spine and Pain is North Carolina’s premier comprehensive pain management practice, offering advanced, minimally invasive treatments for back pain, neck pain, joint pain, and nerve-related conditions. Serving patients throughout Holly Springs, Raleigh, Durham, Cary, and the entire Triangle region, we are committed to compassionate, evidence-based care and cutting-edge innovation.

Led by Dr. Ankur Patel, a double board-certified physician in Interventional Pain Medicine and Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation, Restore Spine and Pain combines world-class expertise with a team-based approach. Dr. Patel completed elite training at Columbia University, Weill Cornell Medicine, Hospital for Special Surgery (#1 in orthopedics), and Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center (a top U.S. cancer center), bringing academic-level knowledge and innovation to our community practice.

Our specialists deliver personalized treatment plans featuring advanced therapies such as image-guided procedures, neuromodulation, and regenerative medicine, with a focus on restoring function and improving quality of life. Recognized for exceptional patient care and dedication to evidence-based medicine, Restore Spine & Pain is proud to set the standard for interventional pain management in North Carolina—helping patients throughout the Triangle reclaim their health and live pain-free.
